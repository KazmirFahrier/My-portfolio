Undergraduate Thesis

Advanced fMRI Classification

Hybrid 3D CNN and Transformer Model with Attention Mechanisms for Task-Based Brain State Decoding.

Abstract & Methodology

This research addresses the challenge of classifying task-evoked fMRI signals by proposing a novel hybrid architecture. While traditional 3D CNNs excel at spatial feature extraction, they often struggle with global contextual dependencies.

My solution integrates a 3D Convolutional Neural Network for local spatial features, a 3D Self-Attention Block for focusing on relevant brain regions, and a Transformer Encoder to capture long-range dependencies.
